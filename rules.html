Cuties SMP Rules

  • Strength 2 and Speed 2 potions are not allowed. Level 1 potions are permitted.
  • Regearing is allowed.
  • TNT minecarts are allowed, but crystals, anchors, and bed bombs are not.
  • Debuff potions are allowed, except for Weakness potions.
  • Legendary items can only be crafted once.
  • Entering the End is not allowed before the designated event.
  • No hacking.
  • No item duplication.
  • Do not go beyond the world border (5000 blocks).
  • No offensive language or hate speech.(JK BE RACIST)
